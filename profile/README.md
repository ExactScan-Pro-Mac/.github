## Overview  

ExactScan Pro is a powerful document scanning software designed for professionals who need fast, accurate, and efficient digital scanning on macOS systems. The application supports a wide range of scanners, offering advanced image processing capabilities and streamlined document management.  

Developed by ExactCODE, ExactScan Pro provides a smart and automated way to convert paper documents into high-quality digital files. It supports hundreds of professional scanners out-of-the-box, eliminating the need for custom drivers. The software can automatically detect paper size, straighten pages, crop edges, and enhance text quality for better OCR recognition.  

ExactScan Pro includes advanced OCR capabilities that can recognize and convert text from scanned images into searchable PDF documents, making it ideal for offices, archives, and individuals looking to digitize large volumes of paper efficiently.  

With its intuitive interface and high performance, ExactScan Pro integrates seamlessly with professional workflows, helping users create organized, searchable digital archives in just a few clicks.
